Yuwei An

I'm an incoming CS PhD student at Princeton University, co-advised by Professor Ravi Netravali and Professor Tri Dao. I got my Master's Degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University and my Bachelor's Degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University.

I am a contributor to SGLang working with Lianmin Zheng and Ying Sheng, and LMCache working with Junchen Jiang. Previously I worked closely with Professor Beidi Chen.

Research Interests

My primary research interest lies in Machine Learning Systems (MLSys).

On the algorithmic side, I develop efficient LLM inference algorithms, including sparsity, parallel generation, and speculative decoding. On the system side, I design high-performance LLM serving systems, with work ranging across the full stack, including computation–I/O overlapping, scheduler design, Torch compilation, and kernel optimization.

I do both research and engineering.
